Cross-Attention Based Influence Model

We propose a model that jointly attends to manual and nonmanual markers in sign language, enhancing interpretability and translation quality by learning their mutual influence over temporal context.

SignAvatar

This work reconstructs 3D avatar motion from sign language sequences using SMPL-X. It bridges avatar animation and sign expression through linguistically aware mesh recovery.

×

SignNet II

SignNet II presents a bidirectional sign-to-text and text-to-sign transformer model trained on real-world sign data with aligned translations, setting a new benchmark in SLT performance.
